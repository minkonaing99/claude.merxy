## Signals (running)
- **Says "calm/warm/family" but picks bold/warm/atmospheric. CONFIRMED 2/2** (coffee 2026-06-26 stated "calm"→picked EXPLORE; pizza 2026-06-27 stated "family, not corporate"→picked dark maximal). Discount stated restraint ~1 notch; bias variants warmer + more expressive than the brief literally asks. ALWAYS keep one genuinely-bold lane — it has won both times. State the warning up front so the user can override with words if they mean it.
- **Dark, full-bleed photography wins when the product is the appetite** (food/drink). Let the photo be the loudest element; char/ember palette over light-editorial for sensory products.
- Loves **big light-weight serif display** (Fraunces 300) as the hero move.
- Loves **italic accent word in a contrast color** (the "sunrise" move).
- Comfortable with **gradients + glow** (did not pick the gradient-banned restrained variants).
- Pill buttons + soft shadow read as premium to them, not the squared/sharp button.
